About GreenBus

GreenBus is your trusted partner in comfortable, safe, and eco-friendly transportation across Egypt. Our goal is to make every journey enjoyable — whether you're commuting between cities or planning a long-distance trip. We provide a range of modern buses equipped with top-tier amenities including Wi-Fi, digital screens, air conditioning, and more. With a dedicated team and a growing fleet, we aim to redefine public transport and bring exceptional service to every passenger. At GreenBus, we go the extra mile to ensure that your travel experience is not only smooth, but also memorable. Our well-trained staff and customer support team are available around the clock to assist you at every step of your journey — from booking your ticket to reaching your destination safely and comfortably. We take pride in our modern fleet that’s regularly maintained and upgraded to meet the highest standards of safety and performance. Every bus is cleaned thoroughly before each trip, and our drivers undergo continuous training to ensure safe and professional driving at all times. GreenBus also embraces digital innovation — from our user-friendly online booking system to real-time trip tracking and digital check-in, we’re making public transport smarter, more accessible, and more efficient. Our mission is to build a strong network of intercity connections that reduce travel stress, promote local tourism, and support Egypt’s growing demand for sustainable transport options. Join thousands of satisfied passengers who choose GreenBus every day. Experience the future of travel today — safe, green, and made just for you.
